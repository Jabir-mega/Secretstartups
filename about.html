Our Story at Petals & Truffles

Karibu to Petals & Truffles, where Kenya’s vibrant blooms from Naivasha’s flower farms meet the rich indulgence of locally crafted chocolates. Inspired by the warmth of Kenyan hospitality and the beauty of our landscapes, we create luxurious arrangements that celebrate love, joy, and connection.

Rooted in Kenya’s Heart

Founded in Nairobi, Petals & Truffles was born from a passion for blending Kenya’s world-renowned roses and vibrant orchids with handcrafted chocolates infused with local flavors like macadamia and passion fruit. Our journey began in the bustling markets of Westlands, where founders Eve and Silas dreamed of creating gifts that capture the spirit of Kenya—vibrant, warm, and unforgettable.

From weddings in the savannah to celebrations by Lake Naivasha, we craft each arrangement with care, drawing inspiration from Kenya’s rich traditions and natural beauty. Our mission is to make every moment special with the perfect harmony of petals and truffles.
